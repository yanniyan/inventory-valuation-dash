What to do with the “Top Location” KPI

Right now, “Top Location” simply shows the location with the highest valuation. That’s not inherently meaningful unless framed as risk or imbalance. 
You have two options:
- Keep it for now and evolve it later
- You can transform it into something more diagnostic:
- Location with highest overstock risk
- Location with highest aging value
- Location with lowest turnover
- Location with highest concentration of capital
